Reflection

We visited a number of technology booths that introduced us to information technology's practical uses. We learnt about laptops and tablets made for military use at the Dell exhibit, emphasising the necessity for robust, safe, and long-lasting gadgets. We also went to the TecD Distribution Oracle booth, where we learnt about the applications of machine learning and artificial intelligence in business. This made it easier for us to comprehend how AI enhances efficiency and aids decision-making. We also went to the Oracle and Archibus exhibits, which were dedicated to facilities management and corporate solutions. All things considered, the visit was a worthwhile educational experience and improved my comprehension of how technology is used in many businesses.

Reflection

PPG, a world leader in paints, coatings, and speciality materials, gave us an industrial discussion that gave us important insight into how big, international corporations function in the technologically advanced world of today. The presentation demonstrated how PPG manages and analyses massive amounts of data for improved decision-making by utilising data analytics, including data lakes and advanced analytics. Because it showed how machine learning models are effectively implemented, tracked, and maintained in actual industrial settings, the introduction to MLOps was especially instructive. Furthermore, the global infrastructure discussion demonstrated how PPG uses dependable and scalable technology to support its global operations. Additionally, SAP was discussed as a crucial enterprise platform that supports organisational needs and integrates corporate activities.
